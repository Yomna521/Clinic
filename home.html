Welcome to The Clinic

Our story began with a belief that we belong to nature so if we are a tree, our health and well being are the root, our happiness is the fruit and hope is how we water and nurture ourselves. We also believe that we all human beings, patients, doctors, nurses, helpers, etc. share the same blend of joy and pain in our life journey.


The Clinic was established in El Nozha to be one of the first and most prestigious medical care facilities service in the district. Our group of consultants were carefully selected to comprise only the best in Egypt and probably around the world. All our doctors are highly qualified with extensive internal and international training as well as friendly & professional communication skills.


Our staff of nurses are very well trained to provide you with the ultimate care needed. The clinic is situated in the most advanced and elegant medical complex building in Cairo (Health Care City – HCC). The whole basement floor is utilized by Misr Radiology Center where state of the art technologies and facilities are available (High resolution MRI, 3D U/S, Isotope scan, U/S mammography, MR Spectroscopy, spiral ct angiography, mammography and interventional radiology). We are looking forward to both welcome you and impress you with our highly professional standard of care you would expect.




Meet The Doctors


Dr. Meredith Grey

Meredith graduated from the University of Idaho with her Bachelor of Science in Physical Education as a Collegiate Soccer Athlete. She went on to earn her Doctor of Physical Therapy Degree from the University of Iowa. Meredith’ passion for learning and expanding her skills as a physical therapist have led her to participate in an extensive number of continuing education courses, as well as completing numerous certifications and a post graduate orthopedic residency, making her our most credentialed therapist.

Dr. Alex Karev

Alex graduated with his Master of Physical Therapy Degree from Idaho State University in 1997. He spent his first four years working at Kootenai Medical Center, in the Acute, Subacute, and Outpatient settings. Alex gained valuable experience treating multi-trauma, neurologic, orthopaedic, and post-surgical cases. Alex spent his next seven years working at a local outpatient physical therapy clinic in North Idaho. During that period, in 2006, while managing that clinic and seeing a full patient load, Alex earned his Doctor of Physical Therapy Degree from Idaho State University. In 2008, Alex and Derek opened In Touch Physical Therapy and Sports Medicine. In 2017, Alex celebrated twenty years as a physical therapist in North Idaho.

Dr.Derek Shepherd

Derek worked for a number of years in the general workforce, including management positions. Desiring something more, Derek explored the field of physical therapy, first volunteering at In Touch Physical Therapy and Sports Medicine, then working as an Aide. After returning to school to complete prerequisite work, he was accepted into a Physical Therapist Assistant Program. Years of hard work paid off when Derek graduated from Spokane Falls Community College with his Associate Degree in Applied Science / Physical Therapist Assistant. Derek is also a Certified Reiki Master under the Usui System of Hands on Healing.

Dr.Callie Torres

Callie graduated from Oklahoma Panhandle State University with a Bachelor of Science in Biology and went on to earn her Doctor of Physical Therapy Degree from Eastern Washington University. Callie gained extensive experience working in a number of different therapy clinics in Washington and Idaho, covering for therapists on leave.
